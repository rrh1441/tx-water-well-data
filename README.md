# Texas Water Well Data — County Statistics

County-level summary statistics for documented water wells in Texas, computed from the
official TDLR SDR well database: median drilled depth, static water level, tested yield,
and modeled drilling cost per county. See [`tx-water-well-county-statistics.csv`](./tx-water-well-county-statistics.csv).

Compiled and maintained by **[Texas Water Well Reports](https://texaswaterwellreports.com)** — free per-address well lookups and
full methodology at [texaswaterwellreports.com](https://texaswaterwellreports.com). Licensed CC-BY-4.0. Aggregate statistics only;
individual well records are not included.
